**For:** CEO / Founder  
**Why this matters:** Domain authority is the single bottleneck. Content and technical SEO are done. One quality backlink from a DA 50+ site can move a keyword from position 20 to position 8 overnight.

---

## How Backlinks Work (30-second explanation)

When a reputable website links to uknetpay.co.uk, Google treats it as a vote of confidence. The more votes from trusted sites, the higher your domain authority, the higher every page on your site ranks. Right now your domain authority is roughly 5/100. You need to reach 30+ to compete for "salary calculator uk". Every link from a real publication gets you closer.

## The Most Important Rule

**Quality over quantity.** One link from This Is Money (DA 88) is worth more than 200 links from random blogs. Never buy links, never use link farms — Google penalises these and can remove your site from search results entirely.

Only earn links from real publications by offering genuinely useful data.
